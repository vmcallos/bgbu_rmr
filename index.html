About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Mang Berto. My mission is to reduce stress and anxiety of public commuters. We do it by offering them snacks and refreshments while they are stucked in traffic.

To reach my target market, I go to major streets where there is heavy traffic then sell snacks and refreshments to the passengers. It is a challenging task to know the location of my customers because the traffic condition for each major road in Metro Manila varies with time of day, day of week and traffic direction.

I use a data driven approach to address this problem and gain competetive edge. I use data analytics to visualize and forecast the time and location of heavy traffic in Metro Manila. I created a dashboard to visualize the traffic behavior of every major street. It gives me information of the time when heavy traffic starts to build-up and on which direction. This information is vital in planning where and when I sell my products and ensure that my resreshments are still cold when it reaches my customers.
